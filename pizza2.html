Arugula Prosciutto

Arugula prosciutto pizza is a deliciously balanced dish that combines the rich, salty flavor of prosciutto with the peppery freshness of arugula. The pizza typically begins with a thin, crispy crust, topped with a base of fresh mozzarella cheese, creating a creamy backdrop for the toppings. After baking, thin slices of prosciutto are added to the hot pizza, allowing the meat to slightly crisp up and release its savory flavor.

Ingredients

1 pound pizza dough, at room temperature
All-purpose flour, for dusting
Cornmeal, for dusting
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
1 clove garlic, grated
1/2 teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper
1/2 cup part-skim ricotta cheese
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
4 cups baby arugula
1 small shallot, thinly sliced
3 ounces thinly sliced prosciutto
Shaved Parmesan cheese, for topping
