Nicoise Salad

The Nicoise Salad is a traditional dish from the French city of Nice, embodying the vibrant and fresh flavors of the Mediterranean. It is a composed salad, meaning the ingredients are typically arranged artfully on the plate rather than being tossed together. The core components include crisp greens, ripe tomatoes, hard-boiled eggs, olives (preferably Nicoise olives), and tuna or anchovies, though variations abound. Fresh, seasonal vegetables such as green beans, radishes, and boiled potatoes are often included, adding texture and variety. The protein is usually canned tuna packed in olive oil, though some versions feature fresh, seared tuna steaks for a more modern twist. A classic Nicoise Salad is dressed with a simple vinaigrette made of olive oil, Dijon mustard, garlic, lemon juice, and fresh herbs like parsley or basil.
