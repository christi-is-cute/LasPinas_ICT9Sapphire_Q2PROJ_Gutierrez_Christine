Pumpkin Soup

Pumpkin Soup is a comforting and versatile dish that highlights the natural sweetness and earthiness of pumpkin. A staple in many cuisines around the world, it is especially popular during the autumn and winter months when pumpkins are in season. Its vibrant orange hue and velvety texture make it both visually appealing and deeply satisfying.
