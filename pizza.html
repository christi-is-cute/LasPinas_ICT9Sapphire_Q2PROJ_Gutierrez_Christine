Black Truffle Mushroom

Black truffle mushroom pizza is an elevated and indulgent variation of the classic pizza, combining the earthy, aromatic flavors of black truffles with the rich taste of mushrooms. The pizza typically starts with a thin, crispy crust, topped with a layer of creamy mozzarella cheese and a rich white sauce or olive oil base. Saut√©ed mushrooms, such as cremini, porcini, or shiitake, are spread generously over the pizza, adding depth and texture. Shavings or a drizzle of black truffle oil are added to the top, infusing the pizza with the truffle's signature aroma and umami flavor.

Ingredients

1 recipe Neapolitan pizza dough
1 pound mushrooms, cleaned and trimmed
6 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divide
1 medium shallot, minced (about 2 tablespoons)
2 teaspoons freshly minced thyme leaves, plus 1 tablespoon picked whole thyme leaves
1/4 cup cognac or other brandy or barrel-aged spirit, such as bourbon
2 teaspoons soy sauce
Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
1 pound fresh mozzarella, torn into rough chunks and drained
Truffle oil, for drizzling
