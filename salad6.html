Caprese Salad

Caprese Salad (Insalata Caprese) is a classic Italian dish that embodies simplicity and freshness. Originating from the island of Capri, this salad is a celebration of the best Mediterranean ingredients, often enjoyed as a light appetizer or side dish. The traditional Caprese Salad consists of three main ingredients: ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves. These ingredients are typically arranged in an alternating pattern on a plate, creating a visually striking dish that reflects the colors of the Italian flag.

Ingredients

1 cup balsamic vinegar
¼ cup honey
3 large tomatoes, cut into 1/2-inch slices
1 (16 ounce) package fresh mozzarella cheese, cut into 1/4-inch slices
¼ teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
½ cup fresh basil leaves
¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
