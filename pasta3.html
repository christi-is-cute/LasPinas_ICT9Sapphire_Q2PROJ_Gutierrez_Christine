Four Cheese Ravioli

Four cheese ravioli is a rich and indulgent Italian pasta dish featuring soft pasta pockets filled with a creamy blend of cheeses. The filling typically includes ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan, and either gorgonzola or fontina, creating a balanced mix of flavors from mild to sharp. These ravioli are often paired with a variety of sauces, such as a simple butter and sage sauce, marinara, or a decadent cream sauce, which enhances the cheesy richness.

Ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
3 large eggs
1 teaspoon olive oil
½ teaspoon kosher salt
½ cup whole milk ricotta cheese
¼ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
¼ cup shredded provolone cheese
1 tablespoon grated parmesan
1 large egg
¾ teaspoon dried parsley
¼ teaspoon kosher salt
1⁄8 teaspoon black pepper
