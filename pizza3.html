Pizza Margherita

Pizza Margherita is the quintessential Italian pizza, known for its simplicity and vibrant flavors. It consists of a thin, soft crust topped with a layer of rich tomato sauce made from San Marzano tomatoes, which are known for their sweet and tangy taste. Fresh mozzarella cheese, often buffalo mozzarella, is added generously, melting into a creamy, delicate layer. The pizza is finished with fresh basil leaves, which provide a fragrant and slightly peppery contrast to the richness of the cheese and sauce. A drizzle of high-quality olive oil and a pinch of salt can enhance the flavors. The Margherita pizza is a celebration of basic, high-quality ingredients that come together in perfect harmony. The red, white, and green colors of the toppings also symbolize the Italian flag, making this pizza a symbol of Italian culinary pride.

Ingredients

1 batch easy whole wheat pizza dough or 1 pound store-bought pizza dough
1 large can (28 ounces) whole San Marzano tomatoes
12 ounces (dry weight) ovoline-type mozzarella or other fresh mozzarella balls
Handful of fresh basil, thinly sliced plus small leaves
Drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil
Flaky sea salt or kosher salt
Red Pepper Flakes (Optional)
