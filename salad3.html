Southwest Salad

The Southwest Salad is a vibrant, hearty dish inspired by the bold and zesty flavors of Southwestern American cuisine. It typically features a colorful base of crisp greens like romaine lettuce or mixed greens, combined with fresh vegetables such as corn, black beans, cherry tomatoes, red onions, and bell peppers. A standout component of the Southwest Salad is its protein, which can range from grilled chicken, steak, or shrimp to plant-based options like seasoned tofu or roasted chickpeas. Crunchy elements such as tortilla strips or crushed tortilla chips add texture, while shredded cheese, such as cheddar, jack, or cotija, provides a rich, savory note.

Ingredients

- 1 lemon, thinly sliced
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
- ¾ teaspoon dried thyme, divided
- ¾ teaspoon paprika
- ⅓ cup light mayonnaise
- ¼ cup sour cream
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1 ½ cups fresh corn, cooked and cut from the cob
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- ¾ cup diced orange bell pepper
- 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
