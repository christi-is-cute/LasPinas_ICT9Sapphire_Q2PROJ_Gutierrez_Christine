Potato Leek Soup

Potato Leek Soup is a comforting and hearty dish that exemplifies simplicity and elegance in one bowl. Originating from European cuisine, it is particularly associated with French and Irish cooking, where leeks and potatoes are staple ingredients. Known as Potage Parmentier in French cuisine, this soup is beloved for its creamy texture and mild, earthy flavors.

Ingredients


1 cup butter
2 leeks, cleaned and sliced
salt and pepper to taste
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 quart chicken broth
4 cups Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and diced
2 cups heavy cream, or to taste
